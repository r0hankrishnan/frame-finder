Intermediate players looking for a budget friendly entry point into the world of aggressive tennis will love this one

Pre-strung for added savings!

Introducing the perfect weapon for the rising intermediate player looking to hit with power and spin. It's called the Ultra Team v5, and it offers an extremely user-friendly path to aggressive tennis. Considering its performance, the price is hard to beat. Like the previous generation, this update comes around fast, making it easy to load the ball with pace and spin. The speed continues in the front court, where the Ultra 100 Team V5 is perfect for hitting reaction volleys and put-aways. For 2025, in addition to tightening spec tolerances, Wilson refines its innovative FORTYFIVE layup technology, enabling greater vertical flex for added spin potential and pocketing. Additional technologies include Crush Zone grommets, which compress at impact to increase dwell time and further enhance comfort. The Ultra Team v5 also features a full carbon handle for enhanced torsional stability (think less frame wobble on off-center impact). Intermediate players looking for a budget-friendly entry point into the world of aggressive tennis will love this one.
