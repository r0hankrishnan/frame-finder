Overview

Pre-strung for extra savings! 

 

Ideal for players aged 9-10, this racquet's ease of use is matched by its impressive comfort. With its age-appropriate length and weight, this racquet makes it easy for improving juniors to develop sound mechanics and progress to the next level. In addition to its cool ocean-blue cosmetic, this racquet is offered at a very attractive price, making it one of the best values in its class.

Everything for the Junior Player

For grip size questions, click on the link to check out our guide. If you're between grip sizes, go with the smaller size and add an overgrip to arrive at the ideal fit.

Grip Size Guide

Specifications

  • Head Size: 100in² / 645cm²
  • Length: 25" / 63.5cm
  • Weight: 8.1 ounces / 230 grams
  • Composition: Aluminum
  • Racquet Colors: Coral Orange
  • Age: 9-10
  • Height: 50-55 inches / 127cm-140cm
  • String Pattern: 16x17
  • Pre-strung for added value!
